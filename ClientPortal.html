Marina Coetzee — Certified Rapid Transformational Therapy Practitioner (Marisa Peer / RTT® accredited)

Introduction

Rapid Transformational Therapy (RTT®) is a therapeutic approach combining elements of hypnosis, psychotherapy, NLP, and cognitive reframing to help identify and resolve the root causes of issues such as anxiety, anger, habits, self-esteem, and more. Sessions typically last 90–150 minutes and include a pre-talk, hypnosis/regression, reframing, installation of new beliefs, and creation of a personalized audio recording for daily listening (usually 21 days).

RTT® is not medical or psychiatric treatment, nor a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or psychiatric care. Marina is not a doctor, psychologist, or psychiatrist and does not diagnose, treat, or cure medical or mental health disorders.

Benefits

Many clients experience rapid insights, emotional release, and lasting positive changes in thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Results vary based on individual factors, openness, and commitment to the process (especially daily audio listening).

Risks and Limitations

  • Hypnosis is generally safe, but temporary emotional intensity, surfacing of difficult memories, or abreaction (strong emotional release) can occur.
  • In rare cases, clients may feel drowsy, disoriented, or experience vivid dreams temporarily after sessions.
  • RTT® is not suitable for everyone. Contraindications include active psychosis, schizophrenia, severe untreated bipolar disorder, epilepsy/seizures, severe dissociation, or recent acute trauma without stabilization.
  • There are no guarantees of specific outcomes. Change requires your active participation.

Contraindications & Screening

You have been screened during intake. If any new concerns arise (e.g. new medication, mental health changes), please inform Marina immediately. She may need to refer you to a GP, psychiatrist, or other professional and pause or discontinue sessions.

Confidentiality

All information shared is confidential, except where required by law: reasonable belief of risk of harm to yourself or others (including child/elder/vulnerable adult abuse), or court order/legal subpoena. Marina complies with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand). You have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your information.

Session Recording

Sessions are audio-recorded (with your permission) for: creating your personalized transformation audio, and clinical notes/supervision (anonymized where possible). You may request a copy of your recording. Recordings are stored securely and deleted after 7 years per professional standards unless you request otherwise.

Your Rights

  • You may withdraw consent or stop the session at any time (verbal "stop/pause" or hand raise).
  • You remain in control during hypnosis — you can open your eyes, speak, or end trance.
  • You can ask questions at any time.
  • Cancellation policy: 48-hour notice required; full fee applies for no-shows.
