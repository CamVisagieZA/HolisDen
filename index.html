The Method

What is
RTT®?

Rapid Transformational Therapy — created by Marisa Peer — accesses the subconscious mind to reprogram long-held beliefs and emotional blocks, often in just 1–3 sessions.

RTT® operates on the science of neuroplasticity — the brain's ability to rewire neural pathways — to replace outdated, limiting beliefs with empowering ones. It excels at uncovering root-cause patterns (often from childhood or trauma) that contribute to both emotional and physical symptoms.
